Together, We Protect the Voiceless.

WildCare is a dedicated wildlife conservation organization in India, founded and run by four passionate individuals. The group focuses on protecting and rescuing animals, especially those that are endangered or in distress due to human activity or habitat loss. Their mission is to create a safe and sustainable environment for wildlife through direct action, awareness campaigns, and community involvement. The organization operates primarily through public donations, which are used to fund rescue operations, medical treatment for injured animals, and conservation projects. WildCare also conducts educational programs to spread awareness about the importance of wildlife protection. Their efforts play a crucial role in preserving India’s rich biodiversity and promoting coexistence between humans and animals. WildCare stands as a shining example of how a small team can make a big impact with compassion, dedication, and the support of the community.
